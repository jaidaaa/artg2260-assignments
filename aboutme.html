About Me

My name is Jaida Mercurio, and I'm originally from New York. This is my second year here at Northeastern, and after quite a few major changes, I've decided on a dual major track, Business Administration (Marketing) and Graphic Design. I don't know much about coding, but I'm eager to learn! I own a food company, Fed Up Food Co., for "when your fed up with health foods tasting so healthy." Essentially, the goal of the company is to create healthy, high-protein versions of junk foods and have them still taste just as delicious. When I'm not in class or working on my company, I'm waitressing in Downtown Boston to make a little extra cash.
